TP - Bounce

By Meridian Witt, Sophia Zachares, and Chimuanya Okoro

Our app is called Bounce. It is meant to help people find exercise partners on Wellesley's campus in real time. The app will encourage people to exercise by creating social incentives. The way the app works is through two modes - when you are logged-in or not. When you are logged in you can create exercise events. If you are not logged-in then you can only see the exercise events (this is also true for the logged in user). In addition, when you are not logged in, you can filter the exercise events list by location and activity.

Achieving Course Goals

I believe my general skills in perseverance, hacking, and collaboration will be quite useful. CS 249 definitely my favorite CS class I have ever taken. I hope to take my confidence here back to my study and review of other core tenants of programming. I did not have a clear goal when entering the class other than satisfying a major requirement.
